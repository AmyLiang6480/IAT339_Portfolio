For Grand App

User Interface Design for Seniors

Our team of three designed an app specifically for grandparents to explore the latest trends in food and activities for their grandkids. For Grand provides a platform for grandparents to share links with their children, where they can create folders with a list of food and activities their grandchildren enjoy. Through this, grandparents can view these folders and gain insight into what their grandchildren prefer.
